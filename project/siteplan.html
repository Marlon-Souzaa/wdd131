Site Plan

Site name

marlon.stories

This name includes the writer’s name along with what he does. It is simple and clearly communicates what the site is about.

Site purpose

This site will showcase the author's stories, allow readers to explore his work, and provide a way to contact him.

Scenarios

  • Where can I read his latest story?
  • how can I contact the author?

Color schema

Primary color: #F1B87E - used for headings

Secondary color: #01015b - used for details and contrast

background color: #fcfcfc - used for text background

Typography

Playfair Display - used for headings

Open Sans - used for body text
